
## 解答

**已知条件：**
- 质量 $m = 2\text{kg}$
- 高度 $h = 10\text{m}$
- 重力加速度 $g = 10\text{m/s}^2$
- 初速度 $v_0 = 0$

**方法一：运动学公式**
$$v^2 = v_0^2 + 2gh = 0 + 2 \times 10 \times 10 = 200$$
$$v = \sqrt{200} = 10\sqrt{2} \approx 14.14\text{m/s}$$

**方法二：能量守恒**
$$mgh = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$$
$$v = \sqrt{2gh} = \sqrt{2 \times 10 \times 10} = 10\sqrt{2}\text{m/s}$$

**答案：** $\boxed{10\sqrt{2} \approx 14.14\text{m/s}}$
