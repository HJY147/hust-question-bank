## 解答

**题目：** 复数计算：$z = \frac{1+i}{1-i}$

**(1) 化简复数**

分子分母同乘以共轭复数 $(1+i)$：

$$z = \frac{1+i}{1-i} = \frac{(1+i)(1+i)}{(1-i)(1+i)}$$

$$= \frac{1 + 2i + i^2}{1 - i^2} = \frac{1 + 2i - 1}{1 - (-1)}$$

$$= \frac{2i}{2} = i$$

**(2) 求模**

$$|z| = |i| = 1$$

**(3) 求辐角**

$$\arg(z) = \frac{\pi}{2} \text{ (或 } 90^\circ)$$

**答案：** $\boxed{|z| = 1, \arg(z) = \frac{\pi}{2}}$
